Shine Grab is all about learning how to play Super Smash Bros. Melee on a technical level. There are plenty of resources to learn from, simply choose from beginner, intermediate, or advanced advanced and learn some technical skill!



Why You Should Consider Playing

Due to the finer mechanics of the game and its engine, Melee has more precise controls and movement options than other titles. Between wavedashing, dash dancing, L-canceling, and other technical options, playing it feels like you have great control over every aspect of your character.
